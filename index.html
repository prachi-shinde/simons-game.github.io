How to Play ?

Steps :

    1) First a button will be illuminated with its respective sound, after which the player should click on the same button.
    2) Next another(or same button will be illuminated with its respective sound). The player is expected to remember the first key which was illuminated and click both buttons in correct sequence.
    3) With increasing number of buttons in the sequence, player moves to next level

Example :

System plays - [Red]
Player should - click [Red]
System plays - [Blue]
Player should - click [Red, Blue]
System plays - [Red]h6>
Player should - click [Red, Blue, Red]
